# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be direct, respectful, and technically honest.

Examples of expected behavior:

- focus criticism on code, design, and evidence
- assume good intent
- keep disagreement concrete and professional
- document risks and tradeoffs clearly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- abusive or exclusionary language
- repeated bad-faith argument
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate this code of conduct.
